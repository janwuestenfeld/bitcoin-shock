Methodology — how the classifier was built

The shock-cutoff methodology iterated through four variants before converging on the walk-forward basis. Walk-forward is the methodologically correct choice because shock labels should reflect what contemporaneous investors actually saw — future observations cannot drive past investor decisions, so they should not be used to define which past days were shocks.
